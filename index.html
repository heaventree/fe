RFR: Rigorous Front-End Readiness

RFR (Release‑Ready Front‑End) is our internal quality bar that every local site must meet before launch. From semantic HTML and accessible interactions to pixel‑perfect Tailwind implementations, nothing ships until your interface feels fast, intuitive, and reliable on real devices.

Leveraging Claude (Anthropic) as a precision assistant, we validate patterns, refine copy, and stress‑test edge cases while keeping experienced human designers and engineers fully in control of every decision.

QWRGQWRG: Structured Local Design Systems

QWRGQWRG is our shorthand for opinionated, yet flexible, local design systems. We define tokenized spacing, typography, and component patterns that feel consistent across your entire site while still allowing each page to adapt to its purpose—from lead capture to case studies and service pages.

  • Token‑driven Tailwind architecture that scales with your content.
  • Reusable components for hero sections, feature grids, testimonials, and CTAs.

QWGQWRG: Resilient Interaction Flows

QWGQWRG is our approach to front‑end flows that never leave visitors guessing. From scroll‑linked navigation to energetic hover states and touch‑friendly controls, we design interactions that feel smooth and responsive—even on older devices and slower networks.

  • Smooth scrolling and accessible focus states baked into every interaction.

About FE — The Studio Behind ASGRG

FE was founded by a small collective of front‑end specialists who had spent years building complex interfaces for SaaS and product companies. After repeatedly being asked to “just make our local site feel this good,” we created a dedicated practice for LocalSites. The ASGRG framework, RFR metric, and concepts like QWRGQWRG and QWGQWRG emerged from dozens of launches where we refined what actually moves the needle for local businesses: clear structure, fast pages, and detail‑oriented interactions.

Our mission is to give local teams access to the same front‑end quality that high‑growth tech companies enjoy—without the overhead. We value clarity over noise, accessibility over shortcuts, and long‑term maintainability over fragile quick wins. Every project is guided by a simple set of values: respect for your brand, care for your visitors, and a relentless focus on how design and engineering translate into measurable business outcomes.
